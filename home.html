Frequently Asked Questions

Does CodeLab offer discount price for students? stat_1

Yes, CodeLab offers a 20% discount on all paid plans for students and educators. Simply verify your school or university email address to unlock the discount and start learning at a reduced price.

Can I cancel subscription anytime? stat_1

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Simply go to your account settings, manage your plan, and follow the cancellation steps. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period, and no further charges will be applied.

Can I switch from monthly to yearly billing? stat_1

Yes. You can change your billing cycle anytime. Switching to yearly saves you 33% compared to monthly.

What’s the benefit of the Lifetime plan? stat_1

You get all courses forever, early access to new content, priority support, and a Founding Member badge — one payment, no renewals.

Are the courses suitable for beginners? stat_1

Yes! All courses start from the basics and are designed for learners with no prior coding experience.

Can I download videos and course materials? stat_1

Yes, Premium and Lifetime users can download videos, code samples, and PDFs for offline learning.
